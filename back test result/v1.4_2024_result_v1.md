# v1.4 — 2024 Full Year Backtest Analysis

**Symbol**: AUDCADm# (XM cent)
**Period**: 2024.01.01 – 2024.12.31
**Starting equity**: $1,000 USD
**Final equity**: $815.27 USD
**Net result**: −$184.73 (−18.5%)

---

## What happened in plain English

The EA traded profitably from January through early April — about 84 baskets closed at target (+10 pips). By April 1st the account had grown to roughly **$1,257** (+25.7% in 3 months).

Then a SHORT basket opened in early April. AUDCAD didn't drop — it rallied ~84 pips against the basket over several days, stacking 8 legs. On **April 9**, the forward-check blocked the 9th leg (`BLOCK_ADD`), and a few hours later the emergency DD cap fired at **35.2%**, closing all 8 legs at a combined loss of **−83.9 pips on 46.31 lots**.

After the emergency close, equity settled at **$815.27** — and the EA never traded again for the rest of the year.

## Why it stopped trading after April 9

After the emergency exit, every BUY signal logged:

```
SKIP_PROBE | LONG | base_below_vol_min eq=815.27
```

The auto-sizer formula needs roughly **$956 minimum equity** to open a 0.10 lot base (the broker vol_min) at 35% DD cap:

```
min_equity = vol_min × WC_CONST × pv_per_lot / MaxDD%
           = 0.10 × 44,627 × 0.07549 / 0.35
           ≈ $963
```

At $815, the computed base would be ~0.08 lots — below the broker's minimum of 0.10. So every probe attempt gets skipped.

SELL signals were additionally blocked by the HTF gate (`gate_long=true` for most of the remaining year — D1 was above EMA20 after the April rally).

**The account can't recover on its own.** No trades → no profit → equity stays at $815 forever.

## Root cause: 2024 was a bad year for this SHORT bias

The strategy entered 2024 strongly bearish on AUDCAD (D1 below EMA20 for most of Q1), so SHORTs dominated and closed cleanly. But **April 2024 saw a sharp AUDCAD reversal** — the pair rallied from ~0.882 to 0.900+ in a matter of days. The SHORT basket was caught mid-grid with no way out except the emergency stop.

---

## Key issues to investigate

1. **The "ruined state" problem** — once emergency triggers and equity drops below the vol_min threshold (~$963 here), the EA is permanently frozen. There's no built-in recovery mode or partial restart. This is a structural design risk.

2. **2024 vs 2025 performance gap** — 2025 run (v1.3 v4) returned +73.58%. 2024 returned −18.5%. The big difference: AUDCAD had a strong upward spike in April 2024 that caught a SHORT basket. Worth checking if the D1 HTF gate would have blocked the fatal basket if conditions were right.

3. **HTF gate was ON for this run** — yet the gate allowed the SHORT to open. At the time of the fatal short open, D1 was above EMA20 (`gate_long=true, gate_short=false`) by April 9, but the basket must have opened earlier in April when D1 was still `gate_short=true`. The gate flipped mid-basket — adds can't be blocked by the gate.

4. **Lot sizing grew with equity** — by March/April, the autosize bumped base from 0.10 to 0.11 (equity crossed ~$1,050), which made the emergency much larger. The same runaway scenario at 0.10 base would lose less.

5. **35% DD cap may be too loose for volatile months** — the 2025 run had 1 emergency too and still recovered (+73%). 2024's emergency happened earlier and equity dropped below the restart threshold. A tighter cap (e.g. 25–30%) would reduce per-emergency damage but may trigger more frequently.

---

## Verdict

The 2024 full-year run exposes a critical tail risk: **a single multi-leg emergency exit can permanently disable the EA** if it drops equity below the broker's vol_min sizing floor. The strategy made money for 99 days, gave it all back plus 18% in one April event, and then sat idle for 8 months.

This is not a v1.4-specific bug — the same logic was in v1.3. The 2025 pass survived because the single emergency happened at a higher equity level ($1k→ emergency but equity stayed above vol_min floor). 2024 was unluckier in timing.

**Next step**: Re-run 2024 with `Max_Drawdown_Percentage=20` to see if the tighter cap would have preserved enough equity to continue trading after the April event.
