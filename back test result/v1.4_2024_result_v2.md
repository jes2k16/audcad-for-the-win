**Symbol**: AUDCADm# (XM cent)
**Period**: 2024.01.01 – 2024.12.31
**Starting equity**: $1,000 USD
**Final equity**: $934.97 USD
**Net result**: −$65.03 (−6.50%)
**Key difference from v1**: `Auto_Compute_Lot_Size_Based_On_Equity=false`, `Default_Base_Lot_Size=0.10` (fixed; v1 auto-scaled up to 0.11 by April)

---

## What happened in plain English

With the lot size pinned at 0.10 (no equity-scaling), the EA traded profitably from January through mid-July — 150 baskets closed at target. The fixed lot meant the same April basket that triggered an emergency in v1 survived here: 8 legs formed, L9 was blocked by the DD forward check, and price reversed the very next day closing the basket at TP (+12.9 pips).

Trading continued through Q2 and into Q3. In mid-July, AUDCAD began a sharp multi-week drop while the EA was holding a LONG basket. The basket stacked **9 active legs** (L1–L9) before L10 was blocked by the forward check. On **July 25**, the emergency DD cap fired at 35.1%, closing all 9 legs at a combined loss of **−85.6 pips on 52.90 lots**.

After the emergency, equity settled at **~$935** — below the vol_min sizing floor (~$963). The EA never traded again for the rest of the year.

## Why it stopped trading after July 25

After the emergency exit, every BUY and SELL signal logged:

```
SKIP_PROBE | base_below_vol_min eq=934.97
```

The fit-check formula needs roughly **$963 minimum equity** to confirm that a fixed 0.10 lot can absorb the full 10-leg ladder under 35% DD:

```
min_equity = vol_min × WC_CONST × pv_per_lot / MaxDD%
           = 0.10 × 44,627 × 0.07549 / 0.35
           ≈ $963
```

At ~$935, the fit-check fails and every probe is skipped. **The account cannot recover on its own.**

The cap correctly prevented a catastrophic blowup. Price did eventually recover to TP on Aug 19, but no strategy can hold through 75% DD.

**Contrast with April**: April's emergency was at the absolute top — exiting was purely bad luck. July's emergency was protective — exiting was mechanically correct. Both still resulted in the same permanent freeze outcome.

## Root cause: July 2024 AUDCAD drop

AUDCAD peaked above 0.925 in mid-July and then fell ~220 pips over 10 days. The LONG basket opened right at the top and couldn't recover. With 9 legs active, the position size (52.90 lots) was large enough to push floating loss past 35% equity.

Key aggravating factors:
- **9 legs = 52.90 total lots** — deeper than the v1 April emergency (8 legs, 46.31 lots)
- **Peak equity ~$1,263** gave more room than $1,098 (April) — but the 9-leg position was proportionally larger
- **Sustained directional drop** — price fell steadily for 10 days with no counter-moves to close the basket

## Key issues

1. **Fixed lot trades more legs before emergency** — without equity scaling, the EA can't reduce position size when equity dips. A basket that would not have reached L9 with a tighter auto-sized base can go all the way to L9 with fixed 0.10, accumulating more lots by the time the cap fires.

2. **Same structural freeze** — once equity drops below ~$963, the EA halts regardless of auto vs fixed lot mode. The fit-check blocks probes even with fixed 0.10, because the full 10-leg ladder still exceeds 35% of $935.

3. **July was unavoidable with current signals** — the HTF gate (D1 EMA20) was `gate_long=true` when the probe opened, so the strategy correctly identified the D1 trend. The issue is that a strong intra-trend pullback of 220 pips overwhelmed the 22-pip grid before it could recover.

4. **No recovery path** — same as v1: no built-in mechanism to restart after equity drops below the sizing floor.

---

## Verdict

Fixing the lot size at 0.10 is a meaningful improvement over v1 for 2024: the result improves from −18.5% to −6.5%, and trading extends 3.5 months longer. The critical April near-miss survived cleanly.

However, the same fundamental tail risk remains — a single multi-leg emergency can permanently freeze the account. The July event showed that fixed lots can reach L9 (vs L8 in v1), potentially accumulating more total exposure by the time the cap fires.

**Next step**: Re-run 2024 with `Max_Drawdown_Percentage=20` (both auto and fixed) to see if a tighter cap limits per-emergency damage enough to keep equity above the $963 floor.
